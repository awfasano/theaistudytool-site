AIcademy Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

Overview

AIcademy is an education app that helps users generate study materials, review saved content, and ask follow-up questions with AI assistance. This Privacy Policy explains what information AIcademy collects, how it is used, and the choices users have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address and basic profile details.
  • Study content you choose to submit, including prompts, pasted text, saved questions, and uploaded study materials.
  • Subscription and purchase status needed to provide Premium access.
  • Basic usage and diagnostic information to keep the app working and improve reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To generate flashcards, quizzes, grammar corrections, and other study material.
  • To save your history and make your study content available across sessions.
  • To manage subscriptions, restore purchases, and provide Premium features.
  • To support the app, respond to issues, and improve performance.

Third-Party Services

AIcademy may use third-party services for authentication, cloud data storage, subscription management, and AI-powered content generation. Those services process data only as needed to provide the app’s features.

Data Retention

Saved study content and account data are retained while your account remains active, unless you delete the content or request account deletion where available in the app.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or remove saved content inside the app where those controls are available.
  • You can restore or manage subscriptions through Apple’s App Store account settings.
  • You can contact support if you need help with account or privacy questions.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@theaistudytool.com.
