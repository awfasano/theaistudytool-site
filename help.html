AIcademy Support

Help for billing, subscriptions, account issues, and study-tool troubleshooting.

Quick Help

  • Make sure you are signed in before generating study content or loading saved history.
  • If Premium does not refresh, reopen the app and use Restore Purchases from the paywall.
  • If a generation fails, check your network connection and try again with a shorter prompt or fewer attachments.
  • Past Questions and generated material should remain available after relaunch when you are signed into the same account.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore Premium?

Open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original subscription.

Why can’t I generate study content?

Generation can fail if the network connection drops, the prompt is empty, or the service is temporarily unavailable. Try again with a shorter prompt and verify you are signed in.

How do I ask Carlisle questions?

Use the Carlisle button from study and review screens. Free accounts have a limited number of Carlisle questions, while Premium expands access.
